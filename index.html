Thank you for using Calculator - Unit Converter ("the App"), developed by 2TDP Developer ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Calculator - Unit Converter is a fully offline app. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The App does not require an account, login, or any form of registration.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address, Android ID)
  • Location data (GPS or network-based)
  • Contacts, photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Browsing or search history

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data only on your device using local device storage (SharedPreferences and local database). This data never leaves your device:

  • Calculation History — past expressions and results you have entered
  • App Preferences — your selected angle mode (Deg/Rad) and keyboard layout preference

You can delete your calculation history at any time from within the App (History screen → Delete All). Uninstalling the App will permanently remove all locally stored data.

4. App Permissions

The App requests no sensitive permissions. It does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, storage files, or any other sensitive device resource.

Only standard system permissions required for basic app operation (such as displaying on screen) are used. No dangerous permissions as defined by Android are requested.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs. The App is completely free with no advertisements.

The only external service involved is the Google Play Store — the platform through which you download and update the App. Google Play's own privacy policy applies to that interaction: policies.google.com/privacy .

The App is suitable for users of all ages, including children. Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data held by us that you would need to access, correct, or delete. All App data is under your direct control on your own device.
